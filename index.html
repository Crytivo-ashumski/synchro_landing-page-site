The city is lying to you

Synchro is a tactical horror RPG about uncovering the truth behind a corporate-ruled dystopia built on propaganda, control, and monstrous human experiments.

You are a young resistance leader whose family was destroyed by the people running the system. Recruit citizens with hidden potential, expose threats with your reality scanner, and fight back against androids, enforcers, and monsters hiding in plain sight.

Battles unfold through synchronized turns: your squad and the enemy plan separately, then act at the same time. Victory comes from prediction, positioning, and reading what your enemies are about to do.

Explore the darkest corners of a broken future, build a secret headquarters, and turn scattered survivors into a rebel organization strong enough to confront the evil.
