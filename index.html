Corporate lies end here

Synchro is a turn-based RPG about confronting a sinister corporation conducting monstrous experiments on people.

You are a young resistance leader whose parents were killed by covetous technocrats. Recruit citizens with dormant heroic potential, read minds with telepathy, and outsmart androids, mafia enforcers, and creepy mutants.

Explore the shady corners of Solar-30, build a secret headquarters, and turn scattered survivors into a team capable of breaking the machine.
